about me

I am a machine learning researcher specializing in continual domain adaptation, foundational models, and physics-informed neural networks. My past work includes applications in intelligent maintenance systems and nano-photonics which were conducted at A*STAR Singapore under the supervision of Mohamed Ragab, Emad Eldeen Ahmed, and Omar Abdelrahman. I currently hold a B.Sc in physics and a M.Sc in data analytics from Hokkaido University Japan and Nanyang Technological University Singapore respectively.

When I'm not tearing my hair on bad models (or other experiments!), you'll find me playing my guitar or catching up on my favorite vtubers. I'm on a quest to compose my own song, even if I'm a work in progress!
